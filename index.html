Chromosome-Count

Chromosome-Count was my debut project in computer vision during my biology undergraduate studies. Developed using Python, it utilized advanced computer vision techniques to segment chromosomes. The skimage library played a key role in achieving precise chromosome counting. This innovative project marked my entry into the intersection of biology and computer science, illuminating the practical use of technology in enhancing our understanding of the biological world.
